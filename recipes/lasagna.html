Lasagna Recipe

Description

What can I say? It's lasagna. The same stuff that Garfield the cat goes bonkers over. I'll admit it. I like it too but I try to maintain a bit of decorum.

This recipe was handed down to me by my great, great, internet connection courtesy of Google Fiber. I bet you thought it was going to be my Mom or some such. Nope, we were (and still are but we were too) hillbillies. No lasagna. Just roadkill with lots of gravy. Don't knock it until you've tried it.

Stuff You're Gonna Need (Ingredients)

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Pasta sauce
  • Cheddar cheese
  • A cool cat for comic relief

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Boil the noodles according to package directions.
  3. While the noodles are boiling, brown the ground beef and set it aside.
  4. When the noodles are done, grease a 9x13 backing dish and put down the first layer of noodles.
  5. Add a layer of Ricotta, a layer of ground beef, and a layer of pasta sauce.
  6. Keep repeating the layers until you're out of ingredients or at the top of the pan.
  7. Top with the shredded cheddar cheese.
  8. When the oven is fully at temparature, put the pan in for 30 minutes.
  9. If it's not a flaming mess, remove from the oven and eat.
  10. Blow on it, idiot! It just came out of the oven!
